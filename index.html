Project Cornerstone

Commercial Snow Management & Property Maintenance

ON, Canada based commercial snow and ice management platform specializing in mission-critical industrial and institutional sites.

Platform Overview

A high-growth, contract-based commercial snow and ice management platform available for acquisition.

The business services mission-critical industrial and institutional sites across core GTA corridors under recurring 1-3-year agreements, driving highly predictable seasonal cash flow and attractive margin performance. Operations are fully self-performed through an owned fleet and have been institutionalized with standardized SOPs and an embedded leadership team, resulting in minimal owner dependency.
